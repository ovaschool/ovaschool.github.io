OVA 2026 takes place at the **University of Debrecen**, Hungary from **July 27-August
7**, 2026. Get excited!

Information about registration, grant applications and dorms will follow
shortly, but please email us, if you would like to tell us about your
situation.

The preliminary line-up of teachers is below; course titles still to come.
